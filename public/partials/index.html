ABOUT

CGNWC members foster community service activities that encourage participation in accessing funds used to attain higher education through scholarship.

OUR HISTORY

Coconut Grove Negro Women'S Club, Inc. is a Florida non profit corporation based in Miami, Fl. And registered with the Florida Department of State's Division of Corporations since May 11, 1970.

CGNWC has, annually, since 2011 with 12 volunteers and no staff provided 40 young ladies who completed 12th grade and entered college over $53,000 in one time only scholarships. These scholarships were financed through CGNWC Membership Dues and Ways & Means Committee Fundraising Activities, and presented during the CGNWC Annual Scholarship Awards Banquet presented at various venues with the following partners:

  • Renaissance Ballrooms on the Edge of The Gables (1998) Theme: “A Founder’s Scholarship Banquet”
  • Renaissance Ballrooms on the Edge of The Gables (2000) Theme: “Founders Scholarship Banquet"
  • Miami Marriott Dadeland (2006) Theme: "The Unsung Heroes of the African Diaspora"
  • Marriott Downtown (2011)
  • Marriott Dadeland (2012)
  • Renaissance at the Gables (2013)
  • Macedonia Missionary Baptist Church (2013)
  • Holiday Inn – Coral Gables (2014)
  • Virrick Park Gym (2015)
  • The Village House (2015)
  • Virrick Park Gym (2016)
  • Royal Photography & Video (2016)
  • Gals On Da Run Catering (2016)
  • Greater St Paul AME Church (2016)
  • A Blessed Harvest (2016)
  • City of Miami District 2 (2016)

Overall the CNGWC’s support services positively impact housing, education, employment, and job creation opportunities encouraging inventors and entrepreneurship.
